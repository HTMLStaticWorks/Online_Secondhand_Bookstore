How We Grade Your Books

Mint

Unread, crisp pages, perfectly intact spine. Like it just came from the press.

Very Good

Minor wear on the dust jacket, but the binding and pages are spotless.

Good

Visible signs of reading, perhaps small notes or slightly aged pages.

Acceptable

Heavy wear, but all pages are legible and the story is fully intact.
